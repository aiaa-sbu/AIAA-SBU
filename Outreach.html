Outreach

AIAA SBU aims to help Stony Brook students and surrounding schools learn more about the aerospace and the aerospace community through events, projects, fundraisers, and more!

Gardiner Manor Elementary School

We launched some small rockets as a demonstration for the students there and taught them some of the underlying principles behind rocketry and flight. 

Open Rocket Workshop

As a simple and free software, this workshop was a great way to get introduced to rocket simulations.

Arduino Workshop

We hope to repeat these workshop in the future as they were a great success in 2018
