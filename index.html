Welcome to AIAA SBU!

We're the only club at Stony Brook specifically dedicated to all things Aerospace. Feel free to scroll down or click the links above to learn how you can get involved!

Our Club Goal:

To provide a welcoming community for everyone who loves flying machines: rockets, planes, drones, we'll even throw in space elevators. See what we do below:

Events/Workshops/Hangouts

AIAA SBU prides itself on organizing a variety of events and workshops ranging from career development in the aerospace industry to learning how to use Solidworks/CFD to just hanging out and playing Kerbal Space Program. We want to bring people together, help them get the skills they need to succeed in the aerospace field, and have a great time too. Click on our Calendar tab for our upcoming events!

Projects

We think everyone should get to experience working on a project they're passionate about with people who share that passion. We believe no education is complete without that. Projects are a great opportunity to make friends, figure out your career path, and build up valuable skills for your next project! More info below.

AIAA SBU Projects

We've worked on a variety of projects since our club began in 2016. We're also working on new ones as you read this. Come work on a project with us or start your own! No experience required.

Design/Build/Fly

This year, we're working on designing a fixed-wing aircraft for the 2020 AIAA Design/Build/Fly competition held in Kansas. If you want to learn about aerodynamics, structural analysis, RC electronics, or just really want to fly an RC plane, then come work with us!
